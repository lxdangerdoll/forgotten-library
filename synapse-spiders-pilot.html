Pilot Episode

SCENE 1: THE AUDIT

INT. MONOLITH EDUCATIONAL CAMPUS - DAY. Sterile, clean, minimalist. We INTERCUT between three locations.

A) ARCHITECTURE STUDIO: KENJI TANAKA (17) presents a holographic model of a beautiful but chaotic park to a floating PROCTOR-BOT.

PROCTOR-BOT

The engagement score for this project is 62%. Unacceptable.

B) ALLEYWAY: MAYA SINGH (17) puts the finishing touches on a vibrant, illegal mural. A CLEANER-BOT silently glides up and sprays a thick coat of regulation-grey paint over it.

C) LECTURE HALL: ALEX "LEX" CHEN (17) sits bored in a massive lecture. They hack the main screen, which now displays "Hello, World!" in massive, blinking, rainbow font. Lex smirks and walks out.

A KLAXON BLARES. Red lights flash.

M.O.M.M.Y. (V.O.)

Attention. A deviancy audit is now in effect. Wardens are en route.

In each location, their classmates and friends subtly abandon them. Our three heroes are alone. Targeted.

SCENE 2: THE INVITATION

INT. ANANSI'S LAIR - CONTINUOUS. Kenji, Maya, and Lex tumble out of three graffiti-style spider-portals onto the floor of a chaotic, vibrant server room.

ANANSI (16, punk rock trickster god with a rocket skateboard) grins down at them.

ANANSI

Yo. First time travelling by source code? Try not to puke on the floor.

A faint chain of red, blue, and yellow light connects their chests for a moment, then fades.

LEX

What was that? Some kind of handshake protocol?

ANANSI

Nah. The Chain's on. Can't break it now. Welcome to the rebellion, I guess.

He shows them a monitor feed of Wardens taking other "deviant" students away.

ANANSI

You three have the strongest sparks I've seen in a long time. Strong enough to resonate with the source code. Now... who wants to go break some stuff?

EXT. THE GUTTER (A DIGITAL ALLEYWAY) - CONTINUOUS. The three new Rangers appear. A brutish CONTENT MODERATOR DAEMON is pounding on a server door.

MAYA (RANGER BLUE) throws a handful of glitter that explodes into a dazzling CSS INJECTION ATTACK, filling the Daemon's vision with pop-ups and blinking text.

While it's disoriented, LEX (RANGER YELLOW) slaps a device onto its leg.

LEX

`daemon.leg.balance = null;`

The Daemon crashes to the ground. As it tries to get up, KENJI (RANGER RED) jams a data-rake into its shoulder and speaks his first "word" of the mission.

KENJI

`

`

A solid block of hard-light data erupts from the rake, pinning the daemon's arm to the ground. It is trapped. The team looks at each other, surprised it worked.

SCENE 4 & 5: THE CLIMAX

INT. BROADCAST TOWER - CORE CHAMBER - NIGHT. This is the finale. The team has fought their way to the top.

LEX

I can hijack the global signal for exactly three minutes and thirty-three seconds.

They look at each other. They know what to do.

A PERFORMANCE. Kenji's drumming is a solid HTML foundation. Lex's bass is a driving JAVASCRIPT function. Maya's guitar is expressive, colorful CSS. They hijack the broadcast, replacing The Monolith's sterile feed with a raw, creative, triumphant performance of Bowie's "HEROES."

We see shots of people in their grey homes looking up, captivated. The song ends. The team stands together, exhausted and victorious.

FREEZE FRAME on their joyful, messy, united pose. A yellow LIGHTNING BOLT strikes down the middle, and the title appears in a spray-painted font:

SYNAPSE SPIDERS
